Additional Details

General paper preparation

  • Common tools are enough for the card crafting. Like scissors, utility knife, glue.
  • Some additional tools are nice to have. Embossing tools can help your make beautiful and sharp folding line. And corner cutter can make rounded corners, which will make your card feels more detailed and completed.
  • If you don't have embossing tools, you can also use paper clips instead.
  • The paper layout PDF file is in each project's assets zip file.
  • Layouts are designed in A4 size.
  • It would be nice if you printed the card within 220gsm ~ 280gsm. Or the paper stucture might be too weak.
